1. Mathematical Foundations

The movement of the "Main Object" (the collective) is governed by Reynolds' Rules and topological alignment metrics.

Order Parameter (Polarization)

$$\psi(t) = \frac{1}{N} \left| \sum_{i=1}^{N} \hat{n}_i(t) \right|$$

Measures how "locked-in" the drones are to a single direction. $1.0$ is perfect alignment.
Centroid Velocity

$$\vec{V}_c = \frac{\vec{R}_c(t + \Delta t) - \vec{R}_c(t)}{\Delta t}$$

The vector used for global crosshair tracking and intercept calculation.
